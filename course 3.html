About Penetration Testing Course

The Penetration Testing Course is designed for individuals interested in gaining expertise in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. This comprehensive course covers various aspects of penetration testing, providing hands-on experience and practical skills needed to identify and address security vulnerabilities.

Participants will learn about common penetration testing methodologies, tools, and techniques used to assess the security of computer systems, networks, and applications. The course emphasizes ethical and responsible hacking practices, ensuring that students understand how to conduct security assessments within legal and ethical boundaries.

Course Details

Duration: 8 weeks

Prerequisites: Basic knowledge of networking and cybersecurity concepts

Skills Gained: Hands-on experience with penetration testing tools, vulnerability assessment, report writing, ethical hacking techniques, and more.
