About Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is an advanced course that explores the principles and applications of creating intelligent machines. From machine learning to natural language processing, this course delves into the cutting-edge technologies that drive artificial intelligence.

Throughout the course, you will study algorithms, data analysis, and the development of systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Whether you are a developer, data scientist, or technology enthusiast, this course provides insights into the exciting field of artificial intelligence.

Course Details

Duration: 8 weeks

Prerequisites: Basic programming knowledge, familiarity with algorithms and data structures.

Skills Gained: Machine learning, natural language processing, algorithm design, data analysis.
